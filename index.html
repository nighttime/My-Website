about me
Hi. I’m an M.Sc. student at the University of Edinburgh, where I study Artificial Intelligence through Natural Language Processing. I do research on computational semantics and am advised by Mark Steedman. I’m passionate about data-driven methods for models of language meaning, and am fascinated by the intersection of language, cognitive science, and machine learning. Before this I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Brown University. I do independent NLP research and also make generative art in Processing. Check out my research blog, and see some of my projects below. Recently I was the Product Manager at TapToBook, a startup that provides rich text message engagement and marketing to brands like Planet Fitness and other local businesses. I directed the product roadmap and lead development efforts. After much hard work, TapToBook is now profitable.
