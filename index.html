about me
Hi, and welcome. I’m an incoming M.Sc. student at the University of Edinburgh, where I’m studying Artificial Intelligence through Natural Language Processing. Before this I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Brown University. I’m passionate about deep learning models for language understanding built on crossover research in cognitive science. I want to build computer models based on insights from the human brain. Most recently I was the Product Manager at TapToBook, a startup that provides text message marketing to local businesses. I directed the product roadmap: I identified business needs, researched solutions, and prioritized development efforts. I developed key technologies for customer engagement, and also for new customer acquisition with full TCPA legal compliance. Further, I scaled the business to serve national brands such as Planet Fitness. TapToBook is now well on its way to profitability. I conduct independent NLP research on the side, and also enjoy making procedural abstract art in Processing. Check out my research blog, and see some of my work below :)
