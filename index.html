about me
Hi. I’m Nick, and I’m thrilled you’re here. I like making things. I also like thinking about the future. Why? Because I believe that tomorrow's world is what we make of today. I care about the experience of being human in the 21st century, which means I think a lot about technology. How does a technology work? What can we do with it? What's the bigger picture? I’m a senior at Brown University, where I major in computer science. My favorite topics are artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction. I also study rendering techniques for computer graphics, digital design, and architecture. Last semester I was in the UK, studying at the University of Edinburgh. I explored culture throughout Europe and loved every second of it. I like sci-fi, internet memes, writing stories, and cooking great food. I'm also addicted to consumer tech articles, and if you like sarcasm, we’ll get along just fine.
