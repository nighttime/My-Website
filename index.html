about me
Hi. I’m a Ph.D. student advised by Professor Mark Steedman at the University of Edinburgh, where I research techniques in Natural Language Understanding. I’m passionate about data-driven models of language meaning, and am fascinated by the intersection of machine learning, linguistics, and cognitive science. Before this I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Brown University. Recently I was the Product Manager at TapToBook, a startup providing rich text message engagement to brands like Planet Fitness. I directed the product roadmap and lead development efforts. After much hard work, TapToBook is now profitable. I also make generative art with Processing, and sometimes make apps. Check out some of my projects below.
