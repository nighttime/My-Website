about me
Hi. I’m a Ph.D. student at the University of Edinburgh, advised by Professor Mark Steedman. I research techniques in the entailment and causality of events. My other research interests include event structure & aktionsart, the philosophy of language, and representation learning. Before this I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Brown University. Aside from research, I make generative art with Processing, and have made a few apps. Check out a sample of projects below.
